# Metadatapp Pilot Summary

Two fictional pilot teams connected sample warehouse schemas, BI exports, and shared-drive spreadsheet inventories.

Pilot outcomes:

- 37 stale or ownerless assets were identified across both teams.
- Finance users valued the plain-language risk explanations.
- Setup exceeded the one-hour target when warehouse credentials required IT approval.
- Both teams asked for read-only connector templates and a confidence score explaining each flagged asset.

The pilot supports the need for metadata hygiene but weakens the assumption that activation is self-serve today.
