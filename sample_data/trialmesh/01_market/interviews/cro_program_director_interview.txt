Interview: Elena Sokolov, Program Director at specialty CRO
Date: 2026-04-28

We lose credibility when sponsors ask for a blocker explanation and our team has status but not the evidence attached. People say they want AI, but what they actually want is confidence that the packet is complete.

If TrialMesh works, it should do three things:
- show dependency chains clearly
- separate sponsor blockers from CRO blockers
- generate an export I can send without editing for an hour

Buying signal:
If a pilot shows faster steering committee prep with less manual reconciliation, I can justify budget.

Warning:
If the graph becomes too abstract for startup managers, adoption will stall. They think in packets, owners, deadlines, and escalations.
