TrialMesh Platform Walkthrough

TrialMesh organizes startup evidence across sponsors, CROs, sites, and vendors.

Screen 1: Workspace Overview

The overview highlights open risks, blocked milestones, and unsupported assumptions.

Screen 2: Evidence Explorer

Users can click a milestone and inspect linked documents, source snippets, and open dependencies.

Screen 3: Review Queue

Every candidate entity and relation enters a human validation queue before it can be written to Neo4j.

Screen 4: Audit Exports

Users export an evidence matrix, risk register, and diligence packet for investors or partners.

Design principles

  • Trust before automation
  • Provenance before summarization
  • Local-first deployment when required
  • Operational clarity over generic AI chat
