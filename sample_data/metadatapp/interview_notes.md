# Metadatapp Discovery Interview Notes

Nine fictional interviews were conducted with heads of analytics, finance operations managers, and data generalists at regulated SMBs.

Common pains:

- Teams could not explain which spreadsheets fed board reporting packs.
- Dashboard owners changed roles, leaving stale Looker and Power BI assets active.
- Audit preparation relied on manual screenshots and spreadsheet inventories.
- Buyers wanted a weekly risk digest rather than another metadata catalog.

Buying signals were strongest when the product was framed as audit readiness and operational risk reduction, not generic data governance.
