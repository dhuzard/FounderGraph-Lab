# Pilot Results Summary

## Sponsor pilot: Bluehaven Oncology Network

Pilot scope:
- one regional startup team
- 2 countries
- 14 sites under startup review
- protocol amendment simulation included

Observed outcomes over 6 weeks:
- startup review preparation time reduced by 23 percent
- missing evidence requests reduced by 31 percent
- meeting count for readiness reviews reduced from 9 to 6
- average time to locate supporting document dropped from 18 minutes to under 4 minutes

Evidence notes:
- strongest gains came from linking blockers to source documents and owners
- protocol amendment drill surfaced stale vendor assumptions within 48 hours
- site coordinators liked evidence exports more than graph visuals

Caveats:
- metrics are based on one design partner team
- no blinded comparison group
- adoption required product manager facilitation during first two weeks
