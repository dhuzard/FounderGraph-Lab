# Metadatapp Pitch Deck Text

Metadatapp is a metadata interoperability platform for preclinical research teams.

Preclinical CROs, pharma translational teams, and academic animal facilities lose key experimental metadata between ELNs, LIMS, imaging tools, and reporting templates.

Core claims:

- Preclinical CROs urgently need metadata interoperability to reduce duplicated data entry.
- Pharma translational teams need traceable metadata for AI-ready research datasets.
- Academic animal facilities need simpler compliance workflows for ARRIVE-style reporting.

Evidence:

- CRO interview notes mention that metadata are often lost during handoffs between tools.
- Workshop notes mention duplicated data entry and weak traceability.
- A pharma stakeholder asked for better provenance before approving AI-readiness pilots.

Risks:

- Enterprise sales cycles may be longer than expected.
- Integrations with legacy tools may be harder than planned.
- Data security reviews may delay pilots.
