# Competitive Landscape

## Direct competitors

### Veeva Vault eTMF
Strengths:
- enterprise credibility
- existing pharma footprint
- compliance maturity

Weaknesses for TrialMesh wedge:
- heavyweight implementation for startup-specific coordination
- slower experimentation on cross-system evidence linking
- often bundled into larger transformation projects

### Florence eBinders
Strengths:
- site-facing familiarity
- document-centric workflows

Weaknesses:
- less emphasis on sponsor-CRO-site knowledge graph across handoffs
- weaker narrative for startup decision support

## Substitutes

- SharePoint + Excel trackers + Teams
- Smartsheet-driven PMO workflows
- Notion workspaces maintained by clinical ops managers

## TrialMesh differentiation hypotheses

- local-first deployment reduces security friction for pilots
- evidence graph with provenance improves trust in startup decisions
- startup-specific milestone and blocker logic creates faster value than enterprise platforms
- audit-oriented exports support diligence, governance, and grants
