Implementation Plan

TrialMesh rollout follows a phased implementation approach.

Phase 1 - Discovery

Collect representative startup documents, define milestone vocabulary, and map current blocker ownership.

Phase 2 - Pilot Workspace

Ingest startup packet folders, validate extracted knowledge, and review audit outputs with startup leads.

Phase 3 - Operational Adoption

Integrate retrieval, governance, and export workflows into weekly startup review cadence.

Success criteria

  • Users trust source-linked evidence
  • Blocked milestones are visible without spreadsheet reconciliation
  • Protocol amendment impacts are traceable
  • Export bundle is investor and partner ready
